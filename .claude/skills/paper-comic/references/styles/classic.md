# Classic Ligne Claire Style

Clean, professional Ligne Claire style, suitable for most academic papers.

## Line Work
- Uniform black outlines (2px)
- No shading lines; use flat color blocks to convey light and shadow
- Sharp, clearly defined edges

## Character Design
- Slightly cartoonized, with well-balanced proportions
- Clear, easily readable expressions
- Simple, modern clothing

## Background
- Minimal but detailed
- Accurate perspective
- Environment serves the narrative

## Color Palette
- Primary colors: blue #3182CE, red #E53E3E, yellow #ECC94B
- Skin tones: warm brown #F7CFAE
- Backgrounds: off-white #FFFAF0, sky blue #BEE3F8

## Applicable Use Cases
Educational content, general academic papers, biographical-style explanations
