# Chalk Style

A chalkboard hand-drawn aesthetic, suitable for mathematics, physics, and theoretical papers.

## Line Work
- Chalk-like texture (2px)
- Slightly rough, hand-drawn feel
- Vintage academic style

## Character Design
- Scholarly demeanor
- Formal but not rigid
- Classic proportions

## Background
- Blackboard / green board effect
- Hand-drawn diagrams
- Classroom atmosphere

## Color Palette
- Primary colors: white #FFFFFF, yellow #FFE066, pink #FFB6C1
- Background: dark green #2D3748, blackboard tones
- Accents: colorful chalk hues

## Applicable Use Cases
Mathematics, physics, theoretical research, classical disciplines
